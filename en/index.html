About Me

Welcome. My name is Joana. As a Systemic Therapist in training, it is my heartfelt desire to accompany you on your path. With a deep understanding of the complexity of human emotions, I offer you a safe space for inner growth and self-development—online or in Berlin.

My systemic approach allows us to look not only at individual challenges but also at the dynamics in your relationships and environment. A special focus of my work is supporting highly sensitive people (HSP). I work in a scientifically grounded, resource-oriented way, and with a lot of heart. I am convinced that empathy and a trusting relationship form the basis for sustainable change.

I am the right fit for you if...

  • You wish for more clarity about your high sensitivity and want to learn how to handle it better.
  • You are looking for support in dealing with everyday challenges such as sensory overload or setting boundaries.
  • You are stuck in a crisis or wish for accompaniment to get back into your power and unleash your full potential.
  • You want to understand your highly sensitive child better and accompany them with sensitivity.
  • You are highly sensitive and a parent yourself and are looking for support in balancing self-care and parenthood.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Systemic Counseling?

Systemic counseling looks not only at the individual but also at their relationships and environment. It helps to recognize patterns and develop new perspectives.

What does High Sensitivity (HSP) mean?

High sensitivity describes a stronger processing of stimuli by the nervous system. Highly sensitive people perceive emotions, moods, and stimuli more intensely and benefit from a conscious approach to them.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes. All sessions can take place both online and in person in Berlin – depending on your needs.
