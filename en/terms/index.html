Fees & Billing

The fee for an individual session (50 minutes) is 80 €. A double session (100 minutes) is available for 160 €. Counseling is a private service (not covered by public health insurance). After our session, you will conveniently receive an invoice via email.

If you have a low income, please feel free to speak to me – we will find a solution.

Our Agreement

  • Everything we discuss is subject to the strictest confidentiality.
  • Your participation is, of course, voluntary and at your own responsibility.
  • If you are unable to keep an appointment, please let me know at least 24 hours in advance. Otherwise, I would unfortunately have to charge you for the session.
  • Important Note: My counseling does not replace medical treatment. In case of acute crises, please contact the medical on-call service (Tel: 116 117) or the Berlin Crisis Service.
